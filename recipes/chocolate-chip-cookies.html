Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

Chocolate chip cookies are soft on the inside with lightly crisp edges and filled with rich chocolate chips. They are a timeless dessert enjoyed by people of all ages. These cookies are easy to make and perfect for beginners who want to practice basic baking skills.

Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar
  • White sugar
  • Egg
  • Vanilla extract
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven.
  2. Cream the butter and sugars together.
  3. Mix in the egg and vanilla extract.
  4. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Scoop portions of dough onto a baking tray.
  7. Bake until the edges are lightly golden
  8. Allow the cookies to cool before serving.
