Lasagna

Description

Classic lasagna is a hearty Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel or ricotta, and melted cheese. It's a comforting meal that's perfect for family dinners or special occasions. Although it takes a little time to prepare, the result is a flavorful, cheesy, and satisfying dish that's well worth the effort.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Tomato paste
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Olive oil
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Cook the ground beef with chopped onion and garlic until browned.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, tomato paste, and seasonings, then let the sauce simmer.
  5. Mix the ricotta cheese with the egg and a little Parmesan cheese.
  6. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce in a baking dish.
  7. Layer the noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  8. Repeat the layers until all the ingredients are used.
  9. Finish with a generous layer of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  10. Bake for 40–45 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbling.
  11. Let the lasagna rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing and serving.
